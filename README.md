### 🧠 About Me

I'm an **Applied AI Engineer** based in Rotterdam, Netherlands, specializing in **NLP, Generative AI, and Agentic Systems**. I build production LLM systems that are measured, evaluated, and reliable, not just impressive in a demo.

- 🔭 Currently building **agentic AI systems** with LangGraph, RAG pipelines with systematic evaluation, and exploring MCP-based tool orchestration
- 🧪 First co-author on a **2025 arXiv paper** on NLP explainability, applying Integrated Gradients to transformer models validated by social psychologists
- 🎓 MSc ICT for Internet and Multimedia, **University of Padova**, 110/110
- 🇳🇱 Based in Rotterdam, authorized to work in the Netherlands
- 💬 Ask me about RAG evaluation, agentic architectures, or transformer fine-tuning
